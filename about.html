About

I live in Ramara Ontario on a small farm property where I grow my own vegetables and flora for naturally dyeing. Over the past two years I have run my own small business that offers upcycled accessories and relief prints that I handmake. Im 2024 I decided to return to school through the Indigenous Friends Association (IFA) and expand my skills in web development, digital art, business and marketing, and ux/ui design. The IFA helped me to see that I am quick to understand web development and that I would love to continue to expand on these skills.
