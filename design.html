Sacred Plants

I was asked to create a high-fidelity wireframe for a group interested in creating an app that would educate users on the traditional medicinal properties of plants. They wanted this space to include three key features:

  • Searching for flora through a camera lense
  • An area where users could learn how to properly harvest flora as well as record notes from their harvest
  • A forum where users could connect with like minded people to share knowledge.

Chimmnising Hydroponics

This was an assignment where students were asked to create a logo and business card. We were required to create a large piece of art with a logo that reflected a small detail of that artwork. I created this piece focusing on the Eagle for two main reasons. The Chimmnising community has a strong connection to Eagles and use Eagles to represent the community. Additionally in the Seven Grandfather Teachings the Eagle represents Love. It was very clear in their companies values and mission that Chimmnising Hydroponics has a lot of love for their community I felt this passion needed to be showcased.

Portfolio

Being able to build my own portfolio has been a dream come true. I began by creating high-fidelity wireframe using Figma and implemented my design choices by building the HTML and CSS in Visual Studio Code. After using a number of different hosting sites over the past fifteen years I am proud to be able to say that my portfolio truly reflects my style. It was a great learning opportunity to understand better practices I can use moving forward, where I excel, and where I have room for improvement.
